01. About Me

Hello! I'm Loknath, a lighting designer based in Mumbi, India. My journey into lighting design began when I discovered how light can transform spaces and create emotional connections with audiences.

I specialize in architectural lighting, wedding events, fashion shows, stage lighting, and interactive light installations. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic sensibility to create memorable experiences.

Here are a few technologies and techniques I've been working with recently:

  • DMX Lighting Control
  • LED Technology
  • Interactive Lighting
  • Energy-Efficient Design
  • Laser Light Shows
  • Ambient Light Integration
  • Smart Lighting Systems
  • Stage Lighting
  • Fashion Show
  • Wedding Lighting
